use Shortcut editor from shotcut.org
1. open original.mkv 
2. trim the video like on iOS by move the left cursor bar as start and 
the right cursor bar as end
3. click timeline on the menubar, then hold the video to left corner
4. move the bar from left to right can zoom the timeline
5. trim the video under timeline
6. click export on the menubar, choose hevc as default, timeline as source,
click the config and detect the hardware encoder, choose hevc_qsv, toggle the
hardware encoder, click advance, resolution 1920x1080, color range full JPEG, export the file
